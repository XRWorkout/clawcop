FAQ

Questions worth
asking first.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None. You interact with your agent exactly like a messaging contact — over Slack, Telegram, or WhatsApp. Send messages, get responses and actions. No terminal, no config files, no maintenance on your end.

What's the difference between this and ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is reactive — you ask, it answers, then it forgets you. Special Agent is autonomous — it acts on a schedule, maintains persistent memory, connects to your real tools (calendar, email, Slack), and executes tasks without being prompted. ChatGPT is an encyclopedia. Special Agent is an employee.

Where does my data live? Who has access?

Your agent runs on private, dedicated infrastructure — a server that belongs to you, not shared with anyone. We never access your data unless you open a support ticket. VIP contacts and sensitive topics are stored in your agent's private config, never shared, never used for training.

Why does setup take 72 hours? Other services claim 5 minutes.

Those services give you a demo. We give you a deployment. There's a difference. 72 hours means: hardened security configuration, live skill testing, end-to-end integration verification, and a working agent that's been proved in your environment before we hand it over. Fast is cheap. Reliable is what you're paying for.

What if I want to change my agent's personality or workflows?

Field Agent clients get one major revision per quarter. Special Agent and Sovereign clients have flexible revision policies — just open a ticket. Minor tweaks happen during monthly health checks.

Is there a contract?

Field Agent and Special Agent are month-to-month. Sovereign requires a 3-month minimum given the custom infrastructure involved. Cancel with 30 days notice.

What if I cancel — do I lose my agent?

No. Your agent runs on your infrastructure. If you cancel our management service, your agent keeps running — you just take over maintenance. We'll provide a full handoff document with all configuration files, agent files, and operational notes.
